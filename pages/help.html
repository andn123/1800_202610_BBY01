Help Center

Step-by-step instructions for how to use our app depending on what you are looking for.

Search places and get directions

Search
1

Open the app

Go to the Search page from the navbar.

→
2

Enter a destination

Type the place you want to search for in the search bar.

→
3

Choose a suggestion

Select a recommended place from the dropdown list and the travel mode.

→
4

View the result

The selected destination will be shown on the map and can be used for directions.

Post a crowd report

Crowd Report
1

Open the app

Start from the Home page or Search page.

→
2

Go to the report page

At the bottom of the page in search page, you will be able to see the options to view or post crowd-level information.

→
3

Fill in the report details

Enter the location, choose the crowd level, and add any other required information.

→
4

Submit your report

Press the submit button so your crowd report appears in the app.
