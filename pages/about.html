About Us

Our team BBY-01 is developing a navigation app to help Vancouver residents and visitors have a better navigation experience during the World Cup by adding features current navigation apps lack, such as crowd visibility and better usability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the navigation experience for both Vancouver residents and international visitors during large-scale events. We aim to reduce stress, save time, and improve safety by providing smarter, clearer, and more event-aware navigation tools.

What Our App Does

Smart Event Navigation

Provides optimized routes that account for road closures, transit changes, and high-traffic areas during major events.

Real-Time Crowd Visibility

Displays live crowd density information around stadiums, transit stations, and popular attractions so users can avoid heavily congested areas.

Transit & Multi-Route Options

Offers alternative travel options (walking, driving, transit) with recommendations based on congestion levels.

User-Friendly Designs

Built with simplicity and clarity in mind, ensuring quick access to important information during busy and high-pressure situations.
